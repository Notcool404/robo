## Practical 5: Empty Gmail Folder Using Recording
### AIM:88
Use App/Web Recorder to automate deleting emails from Gmail's trash.

### Steps:
1. Open **UiPath Studio**, click **Process**, name it **Practical1C**, and click **Create**.
2. Open **Google Chrome** and navigate to [Gmail](https://mail.google.com/).
3. In UiPath, go to **Design Tab** â†’ **App/Web Recording**.
4. Click **Start Recording**, then switch to Chrome and log in.
5. Click on **More** in Gmail, then **Bin**, and click **Empty Bin now**.
6. Click **Esc** twice or click **Save** in the recorder window.
7. Click **Run File** to execute the project.
