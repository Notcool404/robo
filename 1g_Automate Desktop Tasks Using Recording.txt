## Practical 7: Automate Desktop Tasks Using Recording
### AIM:
Use Desktop Recorder to empty the Recycle Bin.

### Steps:
1. Open **UiPath Studio**, click **Process**, name it **Desktop Recording**, and click **Create**.
2. Click **App/Web Recorder** and start recording.
3. Click on the **Recycle Bin** icon and select **Double Click**.
4. Click **Empty Recycle Bin**, then click **Yes**.
5. Click **Save Recording** and then **Run File**.
