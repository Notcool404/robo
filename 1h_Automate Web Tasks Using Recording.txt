
Practical 8: Automate Web Tasks Using Recording
AIM:
Use Web Recorder to search for "Lavender flower" on Google and open an image.

### Steps:
1. Open **UiPath Studio**, click **Process**, name it **Web Recording**, and click **Create**.
2. Open **Google Chrome** and keep it in the background.
3. Click **Web Recorder**, then **Start Recording**.
4. Click inside the **Google Search Bar**, then **Type Into** "Lavender flower".
5. Click **Search**, wait for the results, then click on **Images**.
6. Click on any image, then save and run the recording.
