## Practical 6: Automate a Process Using Basic Recording
### AIM:
Record a basic sequence in Notepad using UiPath.

### Steps:
1. Open **UiPath Studio**, click **Process**, name it **Practical4A**, and click **Create**.
2. Open **Notepad** and keep it in the background.
3. Click **App/Web Recorder**, then **Start Recording**.
4. Click inside **Notepad**, then **Type Into** "This is a basic recording example".
5. Click **Save Recording** and then **Run File**.
